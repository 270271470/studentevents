Connecting New Zealand's Students Through Unforgettable Events

Our mission is to bring students together from all over New Zealand, creating opportunities for learning, networking, and having fun. From outdoor adventures to academic seminars, we organize events that cater to the diverse interests of students across the country. Join us as we build a vibrant community and create lasting memories.

June 2020

Launch and First Event

We launched Student Events in early 2020, kicking off with our inaugural event, the 'Welcome Week Fiesta,' which saw an amazing turnout of students from various universities.

Dec 2021

Expansion and Collaborations

In 2021, we expanded our reach by partnering with student organizations across New Zealand, organizing events like the 'Winter Sports Carnival' and 'Summer Tech Bootcamp,' fostering collaboration and learning.

Feb 2022

Virtual Events and Increased Engagement

Due to the ongoing global situation, 2022 saw us adapting with the times by hosting a series of successful virtual events, including webinars, online workshops, and virtual meetups, keeping the student community connected.

Mar 2023

Record-Breaking Attendance

This year was a landmark for us as we saw record-breaking attendance at our events. Highlights include the 'National Student Conference' and 'Eco-Friendly Initiatives Week,' drawing students from all corners of the country.
